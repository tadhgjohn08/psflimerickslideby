About Us

Established in 2024, Pedigo Submission Fighting Limerick — The Limerick Slideby — has rapidly become one of the most successful grappling teams in Ireland. Led by head coach Shane Sweeney, the team is an official extension of Pedigo Submission Fighting HQ in Mount Vernon, Illinois. In a short time, the team has won numerous competition team trophies across the country — more than any other team from Limerick — establishing itself as a dominant force in Irish grappling. We specialise in No-Gi Jiu Jitsu, wrestling, and competition-focused training in a high-performance environment.
